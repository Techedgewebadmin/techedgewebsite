## Client Overview

**K.C. Thimmashetty** is a homeowner in Bengaluru, Karnataka, with a typical urban residential rooftop — a mix of sloped metal sheeting and open flat area. Rising electricity bills from BESCOM and a desire for long-term energy independence led him to explore rooftop solar.

---

## Project Background

Bengaluru households in 2021 were facing increasing BESCOM tariffs, and residential solar adoption was accelerating. With a well-suited rooftop orientation and consistent daytime usage patterns, the property was an ideal candidate for a 5 kWp on-grid system.

The client was new to solar and needed a team that could guide him through the entire journey — from initial assessment to post-installation support. Techedge handled everything.

---

## Solution Delivered

A **5 kWp on-grid (grid-tied) rooftop solar PV system** was installed in February 2021, designed to offset the majority of the home's electricity consumption during daylight hours.

**System Highlights:**
- **System Size:** 5 kWp
- **System Type:** On-Grid (Grid-Connected with Net Metering)
- **Installation Date:** February 2021
- **Project Cost:** ₹2,50,000
- **Application:** Household lighting, fans, appliances, water pump
- **Roof Type:** Sloped metal sheet + open terrace
- **Monitoring:** Mobile app-based real-time generation tracking

Panels were installed across two roof sections to work around obstructions and maximise total generation. Cable management was carefully planned for a clean, durable finish.
