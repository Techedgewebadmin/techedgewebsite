## Client Overview

**Shri Durgha Fuels** is an HPCL-authorised retail fuel outlet located in Sagara, Shimoga district, Karnataka. Operating round the clock, the station powers fuel dispensing pumps, canopy lighting, office equipment, and ancillary loads — all drawing significantly from the BESCOM grid.

**Contact:** Mr. Raghavendra M — (+91) 98454 15175

---

## Project Background

Rising electricity tariffs in the Shimoga zone were adding significant pressure to the station's monthly operating costs. With large daytime loads that aligned perfectly with solar generation hours, Shri Durgha Fuels was an ideal candidate for a rooftop solar installation.

This project is **Techedge's first solar EPC installation** — the project that validated our execution model and set the quality benchmark for every project that followed.

---

## Solution Delivered

A **15 kWp grid-connected rooftop solar PV system** was designed and installed to maximise generation from the available rooftop area.

**System Highlights:**
- **System Size:** 15 kWp
- **System Type:** On-Grid (Grid-Connected)
- **Installation Date:** 25 July 2019
- **Application:** Fuel station pumps, canopy lighting, and office loads
- **Monitoring:** Real-time generation monitoring via mobile app

The system was designed for maximum yield in Shimoga's high-irradiation climate, with panel orientation and tilt optimised for year-round performance.
